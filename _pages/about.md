I am a Doctoral Research Fellow in Philosophy at the University of Oslo. I am supervised by Professor Oystein Linnebo and my project is funded by the ERC project C-FORS: Construction in Formal Sciences under the supervision of Øystein Linnebo. 

My research interests lie at the intersection of philosophy of logic, philosophy of mathematics, and metaphysics. I am particularly interested in questions concerning the problem of absolute generality, the semantics of Mathematics, and the metaphysics of modality.

My PhD project explores various strategies to define the semantics of potentialist theories, which advocate for a modal and plural perspective in set theory and formal theories of truth.
