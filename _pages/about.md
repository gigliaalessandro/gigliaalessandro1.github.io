I am a Doctoral Research Fellow in Philosophy at the University of Oslo.

My research interests lie at the intersection of philosophy of logic, philosophy of mathematics, and metaphysics. My PhD project explores various strategies to define the semantics of potentialist theories, which advocate for a modal and plural perspective in set theory, PPRs and formal theories of truth.

I am supervised by Professor Øystein Linnebo and my project is funded by the ERC project C-FORS: Construction in Formal Sciences.
