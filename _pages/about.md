I am a doctoral research fellow in Philosophy at the University of Oslo.

My research interests lie somewhere in between philosophy of mathematics and philosophy of logic, with a focus on interpretability, modal logic, and set theory.
My PhD project explores various strategies to define the semantics of potentialist theories, which advocate for a modal and plural perspective in set theory, PPRs and formal theories of truth.

I am supervised by Professor Øystein Linnebo and my project is funded by the ERC project [C-FORS: Construction in Formal Sciences](https://cfors.org/)
